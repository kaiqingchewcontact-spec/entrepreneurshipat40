Your Host

Meet Tori Chew

I am an educator, founder, and lifelong creator based in Malaysia. I built my first web app in my 40s — and then six more after that. Today, the Beanstalk ecosystem includes seven digital tools used by creators and entrepreneurs around the world.

I have a Masters in Psychology, Educational Studies, and Linguistics. I am a certified Webflow designer, a speaker on entrepreneurship, and I lead community initiatives including Taiko for Women and the Pockets of Green Project.

This podcast is born from a simple frustration: the entrepreneurship conversation is dominated by 25-year-olds in hoodies. I want to hear from the people who waited — and then built something extraordinary.
